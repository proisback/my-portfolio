accessibility

44px minimum touch targets. visible focus states. prefers-reduced-motion respected globally. ink-disabled is intentionally below AA - never use for informational text.

known contrast issues
⚠
ink-disabled (#A09687) on ivory (#FDFBF4) = 2.7:1 - fails WCAG AA. intentional for disabled states only. never use for informational text.
⚠
marigold (#F5C94C) on cream (#F5EFE1) = 1.3:1 - decorative only. never use as text color.
checklist
  • 44px minimum tap targets on all interactive elements
  • :focus-visible ring (2px solid marigold, 3px offset)
  • @media (prefers-reduced-motion) disables all animation
  • alt text on images, aria-hidden on decorative elements
  • form inputs have associated label elements
  • no color-only information - always paired with shape or text
what this brand is not

10 things galpal refuses to be

these anti-patterns define the edges. if a design, copy, or product decision reads like any of these, it's wrong.

💜
not a dating app
no swiping, no "matches", no hearts on profiles. friendship, not romance.
🤖
not cold or corporate
no blue-white palette, no geometric sans, no "synergize your network".
🌑
not dark or moody
no dark mode. no brooding. warm and cream surfaces only.
✨
not over-animated
motion is purposeful. no loading spinners on every tap.
📊
not data-forward
no engagement metrics, follower counts, or visible scores.
💅
not precious or luxury
warm, not aspirational. no gold foil, no marble, no "premium" gating.
📣
not noisy
no auto-play, no badge inflation, no engagement spam.
🧊
not a productivity tool
no dashboards, no KPIs, no streak shaming.
📱
not a social feed
no infinite scroll, no like counts, no viral mechanics.
🌍
not generic
Bangalore first. neighborhood specific. never "users in your area".
